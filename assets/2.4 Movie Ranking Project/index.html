Interstellar

A movie about a crew trying to find a better place for humanity to live.
Throughout their search, they find themselves facing challanges noone told them they'd face.
Eventually the protagonist, Cooper, uncovers a secret and it leads to the survival of the human race.

A series about love, betrayal, destiny and great mysteries, throught the lands of Westeros.

Harry Potter

A movie series about the boy who lived, going on great adventures, uncovering secrets,
and eventually saving the wizarding world.
